My son Aaron is 9 weeks old and we decided to do a road trip with the camper to Denmark.

As we were walking on the beach, I thought about something and shared it with my wife.

Everybody online or maybe not everybody but 80-90% of the coaches are advising against beginners.

Don't talk through content to beginners, they don't have money.

They don't want to invest in themselves.

And the list goes on.

The thing is nobody says it online straight, it's masked into "choose your avatar right".

"Your content needs to solve a problem for rich people".

If you join the programs from these gurus they'll tell you straight don't talk to beginners they usually don't have money.

Tara (my wife) said, "mhm, but everybody starts there right?"

And that's so fucking true, most of us start from 0. Except the people that inherit stuff.

So if most of us start from 0 why not focus on these people?

Why should the beginners "be left out".

What's the reason behind this ?

People want to sell high ticket, they want to sell 10/5k to a person, or sell them a monthly 500 subscription.

Beginners are skeptical to this, because what if the promise of the program is not kept

And it's true, I've been in 2k programs and the promise was not kept.

And if feels like shit.

This is not fitting everyone, this is not a one solution fits all.

And in the end... I was a beginner at some point and I started this business from 0.

The first money I made, were basically the money for 2 days grocerie.

That made me so fucking happy because I didn't need to grab money from my salary for that anymore.

That was my motivation.

Let's aim for a week of groceries, for a month, let me try and buy my wife a gift from the money I made.

Let's go out and pay with that.

Let me put 30% of what the business is doing aside and invest it into something.

I'm telling you this because I started from 0, I got here not by quitting my day job all of a sudden.

In fact I was scared to do it so they laid me off and then I had no other choice.
